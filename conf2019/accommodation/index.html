Accommodation Options

Hotels on campus

These two hotels are on campus and are the nearest accommodation to the Aston Webb Building (conference venue).

1 Edgbaston Park Hotel

The conference has reserved 40 rooms available at the Edgbaston Park Hotel which is situated on campus. To book accommodation at Edgbaston Park Hotel you must either send an email to Lucy Woods or Matthew Arnott at reservations@edgbastonparkhotel.com or telephone Lucy at +44(121) 414 9780 or Matthew at +44(121) 414 9779

In either case please quote the booking reference code of: RSE19. This will enable you to book a Standard Double room at the rate of £128 B&B (Single occupancy).

Please note that any bedrooms not reserved by 12th July 2019 will be released back on the system for general resale to the public. Guests will not be able to book using the code after this date but may be able to book at the standard rate if there is availability.

2  Lucas House

Lucas House Hotel offers great value bed and breakfast accommodation, with a convenient location on the leafy University of Birmingham campus. The hotel’s 67 en-suite bedrooms have all the facilities you need for a restful stay: free Wi-Fi, an on-site restaurant and bar, and a comfortable bed. Enjoy a warm welcome from the 24-hour reception, then wake up to a range of tasty breakfast options, from a full English to fresh fruit, yoghurt and pastries.
