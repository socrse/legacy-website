Microsoft

Microsoft Research is where leading scientists and engineers have the freedom and support to propel discovery and innovation. Here, they pursue and publish curiosity-driven research in a range of scientific and technical disciplines that can be translated into products. With access to vast computing power, global multi-disciplinary teams tackle complex problems that drive breakthrough technologies and improve lives. Microsoft is proud to be Cloud Computing and Notebooks sponsor for RSE 2019 – Learn more about Microsoft Azure (https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/) and  Azure Notebooks (https://notebooks.azure.com/).
