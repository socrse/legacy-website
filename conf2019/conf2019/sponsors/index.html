Sponsorship Opportunities

For RSEConUK 2019 we’ve simplified the sponsorship opportunities into four different packages – Platinum, Gold, Silver and Bronze.

How it works

The Platinum, Gold and Silver sponsors can choose one Big Ticket Item plus they receive all the usual networking and branding opportunities. The Bronze sponsor will receive the networking and promotional opportunities similar to past conferences.

Packages and the Big Ticket Items will be assigned on a first come first served basis using the application form below. Sponsors can apply for more than one package.

How to apply

Using the forms below, sponsors can opt for a package and a Big Ticket Item (if relevant).  The Sponsorship Chair will be in touch directly with the sponsor contact to confirm that they have secured their choices.

We’ve chosen to open the application form in January to give everyone time to read through the packages, decide on a direction and speak to their team.

Contact Claire Wyatt, c.wyatt@soton.ac.uk if you have any issue with the application form.
