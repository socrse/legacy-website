Amazon Web Services (AWS)

Amazon Web Services (AWS) is a secure cloud services platform, offering a large variety of compute power, data storage & orchestration services to help researchers & educators experiment with, explore – and scale – their ideas. Millions of customers all over the world are currently leveraging AWS to build sophisticated applications with increased flexibility, scalability and reliability.

All our services are available on-demand, typically in a few seconds, and deployed around the world in any of our 66 Availability Zones (AZs) in 21 independent geographical Regions.

Tens of thousands of government agencies, education institutions, and non-profit organizations around the world use AWS, and we’re humbled and inspired by the work they do that we get to support.

Our Research & Technical Computing team will be at RSEConUK19 to learn from you what we can do to make the cloud an even better place so more brilliant ideas can go from conception to experiment to outcome as quickly as possible: because the whole world is looking forward to the results.
