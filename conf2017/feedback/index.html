Feedback

We really appreciate any feedback you can give us about the conference. To provide feedback, please fill in this form.

We appreciate feedback, both positive and (constructively) negative. The feedback from the #RSE16 was really valuable and guided us as we put together #RSE17. You can see how it guided us in this “You said, we did” document, which you may also have seen as a poster at the conference venue.

We really appreciate positive comments, as they help demonstrate to both funders and our sponsors that the conference is valued and important. As last year, we will put together blog posts shortly after the conference which will summarise the feedback we received, as well as our experiences of running #RSE17.

Finally, if you would like to help shape #RSE18 then please consider joining the conference organising committee. Request for committee members will be advertised in December.
