Registration

Registration for RSE2017 has now SOLD OUT!

If you are registered, then please check the information for attendees page, which will be regularly updated with information about the conference.

For those that registered, the fee was £150 per person, which will cover both days of the conference,  including refreshments and lunch on both days of the conference, as well as the conference dinner in the Power Hall of the Museum of Science and Industry and a complimentary drink on the evening of Thursday 7th September.

View the Talk abstracts and also the Workshop and Tutorial abstracts to find what to look forward to!

RSE2017 will be immediately preceded by the Workshop on Sustainable Software for Science: Practice and Experiences (WSSSPE5.1), which will be held on the 6th September at the School of Computer Science at the University of Manchester. Registration for WSSSPE is separate to registration for RSE2017, and is handled on their website.
