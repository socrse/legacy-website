Poster Guidance

Guidance for creating, displaying and sharing your RSE18 poster.

What size/shape does my poster need to be?

The boards are 180cm tall x 90cm wide, so your poster could be

  • A0 Portrait (118.9 tall x 84.1 wide) or
  • A1 Landscape (59.4 tall x 84.1 wide).

When should I put up/take down my poster?

Posters will be on display for the duration of the conference for people to look at in all the breaks/networking time. Please put your poster up as soon as you arrive and take it down just before you leave.

How can I attach my poster to the board?

Posters can be attached using pins or velcro. These will be available at the sign-in desk.

Where will the posters be displayed?

In the Mezzanine area outside the Elgar Concert Hall.

Will there be a dedicated period when people are invited to view the posters?

Delegates will be invited to view the posters from 12:50 to 13:30 on day 2, and also during lunch and coffee breaks throughout the conference.
